## Risk Assessment

### High Risks
1. **Content Repurposing Quality**: AI-generated content quality may vary
   - **Mitigation**: Multiple model fallbacks, human review step, user feedback loop

2. **AI Token Costs**: High usage could increase costs significantly
   - **Mitigation**: Tier-based usage limits, caching, cost alerts, BYOK option

3. **Feature Complexity**: Too many features may overwhelm users
   - **Mitigation**: Progressive disclosure, contextual help, phased rollout

### Medium Risks
4. **Calendar Performance**: Large calendars (1000+ posts) may slow down
   - **Mitigation**: Pagination, lazy loading, optimize queries

5. **ML Model Training**: Adaptive AI needs sufficient data to be effective
   - **Mitigation**: Pre-trained models, gradual improvement, hybrid approach

### Low Risks
6. **Template Marketplace**: Community templates may vary in quality
   - **Mitigation**: Curation process, ratings, featured templates (future enhancement)
